How it works

Our platform simplifies the process in four easy steps. Users upload an image, and our technology identifies the fruit's ripeness level. Additionally, users can access valuable farming tips and a repository of informative blogs.

Capture a High-Quality Image

Begin by taking a well-lit, clear photograph of the fruit you want to assess. Ensure it is in focus and properly framed to obtain accurate results in the ripeness evaluation process.

Upload Your Fruit Image

Start by uploading a picture of the fruit you want to evaluate directly onto our website. Ensure that the image is clear and well-lit for precise ripeness analysis.

Wait While We Analyze the Picture

After uploading your fruit image, relax and wait while our advanced algorithms meticulously analyze it to determine its ripeness level.

Here's the Analysis of Your Picture

Get ready to receive the comprehensive analysis of your fruit image. We'll reveal its ripeness level and provide valuable insights for your farm produce.
